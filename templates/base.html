About Digital Detective

Digital Detective is dedicated to enhancing cybersecurity by empowering users to perform digital forensic investigations. Our platform facilitates the secure analysis of network traffic, malware, and log files, making it an essential tool for modern cyber defenses. From capturing vital digital evidence to analyzing complex data patterns, we offer solutions that help professionals safeguard against sophisticated cyber threats.

Our Forensic Analysis Workflow

Follow our guided process to securely analyze and report on digital evidence.

1. Sign Up & Login

Begin by creating an account or logging in to access the secure platform for digital forensics.

2. Upload Evidence

Upload network packets, log files, or other digital evidence for analysis in a secure environment.

3. Run Forensic Analysis

Initiate analysis using advanced tools to inspect data, detect anomalies, and identify potential threats.

4. Generate Report

Download a comprehensive report of the findings, which you can share or save for legal documentation.
